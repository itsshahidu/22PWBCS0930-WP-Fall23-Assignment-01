Chapli Kebab: A Peshawar Delicacy

Chapli Kebab is one of the most popular dishes in Peshawar. It is a type of kebab that is made with minced meat, spices, and herbs. The kebab is then grilled or fried until it is cooked through.

Chapli Kebab is typically served with naan bread and a chutney made from tomatoes, onions, and cilantro. It is a delicious and hearty dish that is perfect for any occasion.
